# FORGE 2026 — Submission template

**Copy this whole folder, rename it `TeamName_Function_Hub`, fill it in, and upload it to the FORGE Team Submissions folder on SharePoint by 17:00 local, Wednesday 15 July.**

Your submission is a **harness handover**, not just a video. The test judges apply: could another
team open this folder cold on Monday and run your harness without asking a single question?

| Folder | What goes in it |
|---|---|
| `01_Demo/` | Your ~5 minute recorded demo (MP4) |
| `02_Harness/` | The harness itself — context, skills, agents, connectors |
| `03_Knowledge_Base/` | The source material your harness draws on |
| `04_Documentation/` | README, cold-start handover steps, guardrails register |
| `05_Team/` | Who you are and the before/after of your process |

Every `.md` file in this template is a fill-in scaffold — replace the prompts with your content
and delete anything that doesn't apply. Keep filenames as they are so judging can find everything.

*Questions → **Forge 2026** on Microsoft Teams.*
